About Us

Sushsri PG Hostel was founded with a mission to provide safe, clean, and affordable accommodation for students and working professionals. With dedicated spaces for boys, girls, and co-living, we ensure a peaceful and community-driven experience.

Our facilities are designed to offer comfort and convenience with modern amenities like geysers, attached washrooms, high-speed Wi-Fi, and round-the-clock security. Join the Sushsri community and feel at home away from home.
